About Us

COURAGE– BRAVERY & DISCIPLINE

The Moto of ‘Martial Arts Karate Academy NIT Hamirpur' is “To create Peace, which is the duty of a Brave Warrior.” As a saying of a Brave Warrior, "The best war is that, which has not been happened ever". The courage, bravery, and war should be only for creating peace in the world. Bravery and Courage in a disciplined manner used to be healthy for a person and his/her surroundings. Academy pursues to develop the Disciplined Courage and Bravery in its Cadets by the medium of Physical and Mental Training and to make understand the importance of self-defense and Peace in life.

About Us

“Martial Arts Karate Academy NIT Hamirpur” is registered Academy of the NIT Hamirpur and included as Student activity & Sports for NITians.
This Academy was introduced & founded in NIT Hamirpur on May 2018 by Mr. Gaurav Sharma (Senior Black Belt from World Taekwondo Federation & Karate Association of India),
a student of Electrical Engineering Department (Batch 2016-2020) and certified instructor of Self Defense & Unarmed Combat Training. He is the first Coordinator and Instructor of this Academy.
